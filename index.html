ABOUT US

Pizza, Inc. trading as Pizzas's, is a Michigan-based multinational pizza restaurant chain founded in 1960 and led by CEO Russell Weiner.
The corporation is Delaware-domiciled and headquartered at the Pizza's Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor Township, near Ann Arbor, Michigan.
As of 2018, Pizza's had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the United States, 1,500 in India.
